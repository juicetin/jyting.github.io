A little bit about me

My first encounter with the broad area of computer science and software development in general was in my late primary school years - decompiling flash games to create 'cheating' programs in Visual Basic, scripting bots in Ragnarok Online, and tinkering with assembly to bot in Maple Story. Needless to say, I discovered the paradigm of automating repetitive tasks in my more formative years. I am now in my third year at the University of Sydney studying a Bachelor of Information Technology with a major in Computer Science, and loving every second of it. My passion and thirst for knowledge drives me to learn, and develop myself every day.
